\begin{abstract}% 
This paper proposes a new robust regression interpretation of sparse penalties 
such as the elastic net and the group-lasso.
Beyond providing a new viewpoint on these
penalization schemes, our approach results in a unified optimization strategy.
Our evaluation experiments demonstrate that this strategy, implemented on the
elastic net, is computationally extremely efficient for small to medium size
problems.  Our accompanying software solves problems at machine precision in the
time required to get a rough estimate with competing  state-of-the-art 
algorithms.
\end{abstract}
